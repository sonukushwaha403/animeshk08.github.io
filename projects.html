Bargainer

Summer 2018


  • An Android application developed with the intention of helping those people who don't have bargaining skills and can easily be conned or for those people who have moved to a new town or city and are looking for a local guide who can help them to shop for their requirements.
  • The users can signup and look for skilled bargainers near their location. The bargainers can be sorted based on their rating, distance and service charges. A service request can then be created to appoint a bargainer at a specific time and location. Live time chatting with the bargainer is also provided. Once a service is successfully completed, the customers can review the service provider, and they earn a share of the total money saved through bargaining.
  • Tech Stack: Android, Java, Firebase, OneSignal.
